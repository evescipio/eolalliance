About Us

Small Gang PvP

Focussing on lowsec and nullsec pvp, we are a casual, no BS alliance that aims to generate as much content as possible for our members.

Covering primarily the AU and EU timezones, we happily accept inquiries from anyone. No skillpoint minimum required.

CCP: The YC118 Crimson Harvest Has Begun

We're happy to announce that The YC118 Crimson Harvest has begun!

Will you fight for the newly crowned Empress, or will you chose to stand against capsuleers who seek to disrupt Blood Raider operations in the name of the Empire?

Regardless of the path you take, there are riches and rewards on offer. Locate sites across New Eden which will appear in your overview for a chance to pick up all manner of loot and SKINs from Blood Raider installations.

CCP: Rosada Dawn SKINs

We are delighted to announce that "Rosada Dawn" SKINs are now available for a limited time in the New Eden Store, in support of Breast Cancer Awareness Month this October!

100% of the proceeds from the sale of these SKINs in the New Eden Store will be converted from Aurum to real world cash, and will be donated to Bleika Slaufan (The Pink Ribbon Campaign), which is owned and managed by the National Cancer Association here in Iceland.

EOL: Citadel Services

With the introduction of Engineering Complexes on 8 November 2016, citadels will be able to fit modules to support research, copying, invention and manufacture.

End of Life's freeport citadels will be fitted with these modules, to support industrial play to generate ISK for members.
